Social Cause
 
Homelessness and Poverty
 
Homelessness is dangerous and isolating. The majority of rough sleepers in the area of Old Street, are aged between 35-49 with 10 found in this age range. A small decrease from November.
What will be done
 
We want to collaborate with as many charities and businesses as possible, by maximising the impact within the communities in which we live and work.
The Result
 
Profusion Cares will fire its energy into creating better health, wellbeing and equality for people experiencing homelessness or food poverty.
